Why Choose AgriSchool?

AgriSchool is a programme created to ensure that the next generation of agricultural innovators are equipped with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed. We offer a range of courses, workshops, and hands-on projects that cover everything from sustainable farming practices to cutting-edge agricultural technology.

Our mission is to empower young people to become leaders in the agricultural industry, and to help create a more sustainable and food-secure future for all.

We are in liase with the government to increase both food production and its accessibility countrywide.

We are also in association with various NGOs to promote agricultural development and sustainability. and to ensure availability of food products in arid regions.
